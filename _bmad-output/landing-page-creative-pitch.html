12 / 13

Our Recommendation

Direction A.
“The Street.”

Anti-SaaS

Every competitor has gradient cards and glassmorphism. A Sharpie on a real photo is an act of rebellion.

Human

The handwritten annotation says “a human saw this.” That mirrors the product truth: AI finds it, humans verify it.

Memorable

Visitors won't forget the Polaroid moment. It's a brand act, not a landing page. People share brand acts.

The Sharpie annotation can become UpSight's visual brand language—product screenshots, blog headers, email, social. One system that says: we added the insight layer that was always missing.

Key Decisions

Photography vs. illustration? Real photos = maximum emotional impact

•

3 or 5 characters? Start with 3 for focused storytelling

•

Static or animated? Subtle parallax + hover reveals recommended

•

Mobile adaptation? Tap to reveal instead of hover, swipe between people
