Design Direction Comparison

Best for Glanceability
  • Classic Cards (1) - Color borders scan instantly
  • Tag-First (6) - Categories dominate visually
  • Timeline (4) - Dots create visual rhythm
Best for Density
  • Ultra Compact (5) - Maximum evidence visible
  • Minimal Lines (2) - Clean, efficient spacing
Best for Modern Feel
  • Bubble Style (3) - Playful, conversational
  • Timeline (4) - Elegant, editorial
Recommendation
  • Direction 1 (Classic Cards) as primary
  • Proven pattern, scannable, professional
  • Aligns with Dovetail/Grain expectations
  • Easy to implement with shadcn/ui
