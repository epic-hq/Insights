## 3. Beachhead: Consultants & Fractional Execs

### Market Size

| Metric | Number | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **US consulting firms (all sizes)** | ~1,000,000 | IBISWorld |
| **Firms with <50 employees** | ~870,000 (87%) | Consulting Success |
| **US freelance knowledge workers** | ~34M | DemandSage |
| **Freelancers doing consulting-type work** | ~10-13M (est. 30-40%) | Derived |
| **Average consultant hourly rate** | $47.71 avg, $200-1,000+ for specialists | Melisa Liberman |
| **Freelance collective earnings (2024)** | $1.5T | Melisa Liberman |
| **Projected freelancers by 2027** | 86.5M (>50% of workforce) | DemandSage |

### Why Consultants First

| Factor | Evidence |
|--------|---------|
| **Clearest ROI math** | 8-12 hours saved/project at $150-300/hr = $1,200-3,600/project |
| **Buy alone** | Solo decision-maker, no procurement |
| **Fast feedback loop** | Will tell you exactly what's broken |
| **Zero tolerance for fluff** | Forces product discipline |
| **Network effects** | Consultants recommend tools to each other |
| **Low competition** | No purpose-built tool for consultant discovery workflows |

### Decision Rule

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 5+ consultants convert and reuse within 60 days | Invest in consulting-specific features (SOW generator, stakeholder matrix) |
| Weak adoption after 60 days | Keep as secondary, focus on founders |

## 4. Fast-Follow: Post-Launch Founders

### Market Size

| Metric | Number | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **New US businesses started (2023)** | 5.5M | DemandSage |
| **Active startups/small companies (5-50 employees)** | ~5-10M | Derived |
| **Post-launch with product** | ~2-4M (est. 40-50%) | Derived |
| **Doing regular customer research** | ~500K-1.6M (est. 25-40%) | Industry estimate |
| **Startups that fail from no market need** | 42% | CB Insights |

### Why This Segment Matters

| Factor | Evidence |
|--------|---------|
| **Highest urgency** | Need clarity before runway runs out |
| **Highest willingness to pay** | Desperate for signal |
| **Lowest competition** | No tool specifically targets "pivot or persevere" decision |
| **Best activation behavior** | Upload 5+ existing conversations immediately |
| **Clear outcome** | Evidence for board meeting / investor update |

### Hypothesis to Validate

Post-launch founders with <$10K MRR and <18 months runway will:
- Upload 5+ existing conversations in first session (target: 70%)
- Reach conviction in pivot-or-persevere decision within 1 week
- Convert to paid faster than any other segment

## 5. Secondary: Startup Product Leads

### Market Size

| Metric | Number | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Product management roles (US)** | ~300,000-500,000 | BLS, LinkedIn data |
| **At companies with <50 employees** | ~100,000-200,000 (est.) | Derived |
| **Without dedicated research team** | ~80,000-160,000 (est. 80%) | Derived |

### Why Secondary (Not Primary)

- **Crowded market**: Dovetail ($700M valuation), Productboard, Maze all compete here
- **Slower buying**: PMs need to justify tool purchases to leadership
- **Lower urgency**: Research is important but rarely urgent
- **Better activation**: Once consultants/founders prove the product, PMs follow

| Tier | Target | Price Range | Rationale |
|------|--------|-------------|-----------|
