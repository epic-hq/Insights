# Competitive Analysis: Grain

## Overview

**Company**: Grain
**Website**: https://grain.com
**Category**: AI Notetaker / Conversation Intelligence
**Positioning**: "The AI Notetaker Built for Growing Teams" - Video highlights and insights

## Their Key Products

| Product | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Notetaker** | Recording, transcription, automatic notes |
| **Highlights** | Video clips created by selecting transcript text |
| **Stories** | Multiple highlights stitched into narratives |
| **Conversation Intelligence** | Keyword tracking, trends across calls |
| **MCP Server** | Connect meeting data to Claude, Cursor, etc. |

## Their Strengths

1. **Text-Based Video Editing**: Select text to create video clips (brilliant UX)
2. **Stories Feature**: Combine highlights into shareable narratives
3. **Growing Team Focus**: Built for collaboration, not just individual use
4. **Native Integrations**: HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier, Slack
5. **Keyword Tracking**: Track specific words/phrases across team calls
6. **MCP Server**: AI tool integration (Claude, Cursor)
7. **Clean Interface**: Video + transcript + AI summary in one view

## Their Weaknesses

1. **Meeting-Centric**: Only works with scheduled video meetings
2. **Limited Analysis**: Tracks keywords, but no deep pattern analysis
3. **No Research Frameworks**: Generic conversation tool, not research-specific
4. **No Voice-First**: Traditional meeting recorder approach
5. **No AI Interviewer**: Records conversations, doesn't conduct them

---

## How We Beat Them

### Strategy 1: Deep Analysis vs. Surface Tracking

**Our Advantage**: Grain tracks keywords; we extract themes, evidence, and insights.

**Tactics**:
- "Beyond keyword tracking - real insight extraction"
- Show lenses/frameworks vs. simple word counts
- Emphasize cross-conversation pattern analysis

**Feature Comparison**:
| Feature | Grain | UpSight |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Keyword tracking | Yes | Yes |
| Theme extraction | No | Yes |
| Evidence tagging | No | Yes |
| Research frameworks | No | Yes (Lenses) |
| Pattern analysis | Basic | Advanced |

### Strategy 2: AI That Conducts Research

**Our Advantage**: Their AI listens; our AI asks questions.

**Tactics**:
- "Don't just record - let AI guide your research"
- Emphasize LiveKit voice agent
- Show interview prompt generation

### Strategy 3: Research Repository

**Our Advantage**: Long-term insight management vs. meeting recordings.

**Tactics**:
- "Build knowledge, not just a recording library"
- Evidence repository that surfaces relevant insights
- Cross-project pattern detection

## UX Patterns to Learn From

### What They Do Right

1. **Text-Based Highlight Creation**: Select transcript text = video clip (genius UX)
2. **Stories**: Combine highlights into shareable narratives
3. **Three-Pane View**: Video + transcript + notes side-by-side
4. **Click-to-Jump**: Click AI note or transcript to jump in video
5. **Keyword Tracking**: Visual trends of word/phrase frequency
6. **MCP Integration**: Forward-thinking AI tool connectivity

### What We Should Adopt

- **Text-Based Highlight Creation**: Select transcript text to create clips
- **Stories/Collections**: Bundle evidence into shareable packages
- **Three-Pane View**: Video + transcript + analysis together
- **Click-to-Jump**: Seamless navigation between text and media
