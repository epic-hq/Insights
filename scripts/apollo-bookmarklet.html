Setup (one time)

  1. Show your bookmarks bar if it's hidden.
    Chrome/Brave: Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows)
  2. Drag the purple button above onto your bookmarks bar.
    You should see "Download Apollo Video" appear as a bookmark.

To download a recording

  1. Open the Apollo share link in your browser.
    Make sure you're logged into Apollo and can see the video player.
  2. Wait for the video to load — you should see the video player on the page.
  3. Click "Download Apollo Video" in your bookmarks bar.
    The video file will start downloading to your Downloads folder.
  4. Upload the downloaded file to UpSight using the file upload option.
Note: You must be logged into Apollo.io in the same browser for this to work. The bookmarklet finds the video URL on the page and triggers a download — it does not send any data anywhere.
